Ukraine Local Time Details

Time Zone
Abbreviations

 Eastern European Time - is abbreviated as EET
Eastern European Summer Time - is abbreviated as EEST

UTC - GMT
Offset

 Ukraine is GMT/UTC + 2h during Standard Time
Ukraine is GMT/UTC + 3h during Daylight Saving Time

Daylight Saving
Time Usage

 Ukraine does utilize Daylight Saving Time. In Europe daylight saving time is often referred to as "Summer Time."

Daylight Saving
Start Date

Ukraine started Daylight Saving Time on
Sunday March 27, 2016 at 3:00 AM local time.

Daylight Saving
End Date

Ukraine ended Daylight Saving Time on
Sunday October 30, 2016 at 4:00 AM local time.

In September 2011 the Ukrainian Parliament passed legislation that would have kept Ukraine on Daylight Saving Time year round, however, this was reversed by the Ukrainian Parliament on Tuesday October 18, 2011 due to widespread objections.
