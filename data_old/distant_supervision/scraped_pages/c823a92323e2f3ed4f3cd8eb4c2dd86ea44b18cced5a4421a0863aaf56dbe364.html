Lake Bled Resort Guide

Lake Bled is indisputably one of Europe's most beautiful lakes. Find out why...

The Resort

The beauty of the mountains reflected on the lake, the sun, the serenity and the fresh air attracts explorers, artists, sports enthusiasts, the young and the old from all over the world. Weddings at Lake Bled are quite a common site due to great venues such as fairytale Bled Castle that overlooks the lake.

Places to see

Julian Alps Spectacular mountain scenery and unspoiled, intact natural beauty.
Taste of Slovenia Following the valley of turquoise river Soca by train, boat and coach.
Ljubljana Youngest capital of Europe, unique architecture and scenic funicular ride.
Postojna cave & Predjama castle One of world's largest & most beautiful cave systems.
Treasures of Venice A step back in time and visit to Italy's most magical city.
Lipizzaner stud farm Tour of the stables, buggy ride and amazing routine display.
Bohinj Valley Slovenia's largest glacier lake in the Triglav National Park.

Weather

Weather in Slovenia varies considerably depending on area, yet average July temperature remains 27C. Hot sunny days and fresh evenings with occasional shower are regular occurrence in the mountains. Alpine peaks covered in snow in July/August makes views even more spectacular!
