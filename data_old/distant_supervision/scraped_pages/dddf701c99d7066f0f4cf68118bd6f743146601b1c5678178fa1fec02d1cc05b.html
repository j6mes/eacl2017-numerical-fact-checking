    Arizona Monsoon Blows Away Trampoline

    Storyful•13 August 2014

    Arizona’s monsoon winds and rain brought disruption to the Phoenix metro area on Tuesday, August 12. Firefighters rescued motorists from six cars stranded in flood water while fallen trees and power lines blocked roads. Over 5,000 people lost their power supply at the height of the storm.
