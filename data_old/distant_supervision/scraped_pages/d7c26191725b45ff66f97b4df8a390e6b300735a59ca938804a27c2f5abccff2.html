Republic of Ireland v England kick-off time confirmed

The Football Association of Ireland today (Wednesday) confirmed that kick-off time for the Three International Friendly between the Republic of Ireland and England has been confirmed for 1pm on Sunday, June 7 following discussions with the FA, broadcasters, and the Garda.
4th Mar 2015

The Football Association of Ireland today (Wednesday) confirmed that kick-off time for the Three International Friendly between the Republic of Ireland and England has been confirmed for 1pm on Sunday, June 7 following discussions with the FA, broadcasters, and the Garda.

3,000 tickets will be made available through the FA for English supporters.

Irish ticket sales details will be provided in due course.
