About the show

Description

We can help you get your Projects Solved! With thousands of square feet of exhibits youll find the latest in products and services for everything from builders, contractors, materials, decks, pools, spas, entertainment systems, storage, plants, landscape displays/materials, kitchenware, and much more.

SHOW HIGHLIGHTS

Landscapes & Gardens & Plants, Oh My!

Come see thousands of square feet of stunning landscape displays and water features and get inspiration for your own home and garden. Bring your pictures, plans, and questions and talk with local pros face-to-face.Plus,‘Ask The Experts’ of the Collin County Master Gardeners your frustrating gardening questions!

Home Improvement & Remodeling Down Every Aisle

Shop and compare over 150 booths of:

Custom Home Builders

Interior Designers & Painters

Roofers, Windows & Doors
Interior Decorators
Take Home The Inspiration

Visit our HUGE onsite Fall Plant & Pumpkin sale by Shades of Green, PLUS the 1500+ Sq. Ft. Hay Maze! Even if Texas Fall starts a little later, you can bring some Fall home today!

Family Fun
This show has something for every family member! For the kiddos, there will be Design & Color Ceramic Tiles Workshop by The Home Depot, plus the 1500+ sq. ft. Hay Maze by Shades of Green! And for the pet lovers in the family there will be pet adoptions by Legacy Humane Society and Greyhound Adoptions.
Art, Gift, Gourmet & GoTexan

Shop til you drop, you could even finish your holiday shopping early! Youll find something for everyone jewelry, savory and sweet; including pastas, spices, cakes, dips, toffees and more all made by locals and Go Texan Members! And check out the Gourmet Food Trucks on the way in!

*Features and show details are subject to change.
