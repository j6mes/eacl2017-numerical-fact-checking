Born: 1988 in London, England

British-Ghanaian rapper Fuse ODG made his name in the London rap scene after growing up in the English capital. Born Richard Abiona, a talented producer as well as rapper, he has been an active influence in helping young people in his local communities realize their musical aspirations through a mobile studio.
