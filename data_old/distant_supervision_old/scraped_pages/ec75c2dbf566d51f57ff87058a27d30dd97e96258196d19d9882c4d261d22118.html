Event description

Description

Mark Weston, author of the brand-new book, The Runner-Up Presidency. Bookstore1Sarasota, 1359 Main Street. What better way to get in the mood for Florida’s March 15th presidential primary than by hearing about Mark Weston’s book?  Its full title is The Runner-up Presidency: The Elections That Defied America's Popular Will (And How Our Democracy Remains in Danger). If that title alone is not food for thought—what is? Please join us for what is bound to be a great discussion.

 

Mark Weston is the author of four other books including the Giants of Japan: The Lives of Japan’s Greatest Men and Women and Prophets and Princes: Saudi Arabia from Muhammad to the Present.
