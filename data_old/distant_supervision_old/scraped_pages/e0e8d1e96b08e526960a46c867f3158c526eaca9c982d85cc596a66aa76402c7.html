Our company

NATS is the UK's leading provider of air traffic control services. Each year we handle 2.4 million flights and 250 million passengers in UK airspace.

In addition to providing services to 13 UK airports, and managing all upper airspace in the UK, we provide services around the world spanning Europe, the Middle East, Asia and North America.
