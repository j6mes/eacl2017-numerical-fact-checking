Golf at Jackson Country Club

Since 1914 golf at Jackson Country club has been more than a tradition it has been a way of life. We are proud of our Championship level golf course, and proud to have hosted more Tennessee State Championships (39) than any other course in Tennessee. Our Course has been a favorite playing ground for many of the games greatest players, champions, and ambassadors of the game such as: Walter Hagan, Ben Hogan, Byron Nelson, Patty Berg, Arnold Palmer, Mickey Wright, Lou Graham, Cary Middlecoff, John Daly, Loren Roberts, and many many more. Once you have experienced Jackson Country Club's Course you will understand their love for it, why the course stands the test of time, and why it is among Tennessee's best courses.

Jackson Country Club's classically designed masterpiece of a golf course features the following:

· A pristinely manicured golf course.

· Five distinct sets of tees for all skill levels of player from beginner to professional to enjoy.

· Champion Bermuda Greens.

· Full length Driving Range and Practice Facility.

· Putting Green, complete with short game practice facility.

· Fun and Exciting Tournaments and events for Men, Ladies' and Juniors to enjoy.

· Fun Couple's Nin and Dine's.

· Professional Golf Instruction for all skill levels of players.

· Men's and Ladies' Locker Rooms.

· Golf Club Storage and Cleaning.

So, if you are looking for a Club that provides the best golf has to offer in West Tennessee then you need not look further than Jackson Country Club. Kedric will be happy to guide you through the Club and give you the opportunity to experience Jackson Country Club as a "Member for a Day".
