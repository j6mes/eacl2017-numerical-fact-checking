Online Telugu Calendar and Telugu Panchangam

Telugu Calendar in Telugu - Dynamic Telugu Year Calender starting from 1870

This is a clickable Telugu Calendar giving dates and Daily Panchangam of any date betweem 1870 and 2019. Telugu calendar is the traditional calendar of Andhra Pradesh and followed by telugu people. As per the telugu year calendar, Yugadi or Ugadi marks the beginning of Telugu New Year which usually falls in the month of March or April.

This Telugu Calendar helps you find the telugu date of any year starting from 1870. To get daily telugu panchangam, choose the year and month and click on any date. The Panchangam of that particular day will be displayed. You can view this calendar in English or in Telugu. Also you can set the based calendar as Gregorian calendar and view the telugu calendar within english dates or set base calendar as Telugu calendar and view english dates within Telugu year dates.
