Norwich City

Norwich City Football Club website featuring all the latest stats, fixtures, results as well as breaking Norwich City football news. The Canaries play at Carrow Road and were founded in 1902. Norwich City football club play in the Premier League with their distinctive canary yellow shirts. Within two seasons Norwich City football club went from playing League 1 football to playing against the likes of Chelsea and Manchester United in the Premiership, under Paul Lambert.
