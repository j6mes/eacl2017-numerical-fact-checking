List of the hottest female celebrities with brown eyes. These sexy brown-eyed stars, which happen to be some of the most beautiful celebrities of all time, are all listed here in this hottest women with brown eyes list. Brown colored eyes are the result of a very high concentration of melanin in the iris storm. Brown is a dominant eye color for humans in various parts of the world, including Asia, Europe, Africa, and the Americas. Medical studies have even shown that brown eyes tend to appear more trustworthy than their blue-eyed counterparts.

Sure to be tops in this list of brown eyed beauties are actresses like Julia Roberts, Halle Berry, Jessica Alba, Jennifer Lopez, and Sandra Bullock. They are joined by some up-and-coming actresses, pop superstars like Britney Spears, and international beauties such as Freida Pinto and Penelope Cruz. Varying between light brown, golden or honey brown, amber, and dark brown, these girls truly captivate everyone with just one look. Also included in the list is sexy star Mila Kunis, who has a medical condition called heterochromia iridium, which causes the actress to have one brown eye and one green.
