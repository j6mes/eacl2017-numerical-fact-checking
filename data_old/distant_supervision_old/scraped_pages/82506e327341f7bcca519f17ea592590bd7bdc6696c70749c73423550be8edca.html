A Civil War Encampment, the Battle of Cottonwood Creek, will be held on the grounds of the Allen Heritage Village. Collin County Living Historians will be camping on the ground from Friday to Sunday. Activities include medical demonstration, skirmishes, musket firing, sewing on a button, a quilt display, and balloon art.
