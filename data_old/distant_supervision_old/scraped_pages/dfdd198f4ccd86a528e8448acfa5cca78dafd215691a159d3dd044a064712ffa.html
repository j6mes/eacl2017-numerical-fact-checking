Heisman Trophy Tim Brown to make appearance at Allen Event Center

Filed under Allen at May 2013

Tim Brown's Heisman Trophy, along with the ball with which he made his 1,000th reception, will be on display during the Texas Revolution's June 1 game.

Brown, the Revolution's general manager, announced Tuesday that he will be putting his 1987 Heisman Trophy on display at the Allen Event Center for the Revolution's home game against the Colorado Ice.

The first 1,000 in attendance will also receive a bobblehead.

The game will feature some of Brown's favorite memorabilia, including video clips highlighting his playing career. Brown was a nine-time participant in the NFL Pro Bowl.

The Revolution have four Indoor Football League games remaining this season, including three at the Allen Event Center, 200 E Stacy Road.

For more information, or to purchase tickets, visit TexasRevs.com, or call 972-422.-7277.
