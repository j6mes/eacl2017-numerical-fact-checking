Big Job Fair in Allen

Posted on September 28, 2016

ALLEN (WBAP/KLIF News) – The Cumulus “Putting Texans Back to Work” job fair is Wednesday in Allen.

It will take place from 9:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. at the Allen Event Center at Highway 75 and Stacy.

About 40 employers will be in attendance.
