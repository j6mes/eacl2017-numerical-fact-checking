British number one Johanna Konta beats US Open runner-up to reach China Open quarters

JOHANNA KONTA, the British No 1, showed her fighting spirit to beat US Open runner-up Karolina Pliskova 6-1, 3-6, 7-6 to reach the last eight of the China Open.

By Express Sport
PUBLISHED: 00:05, Fri, Oct 7, 2016 | UPDATED: 00:16, Fri, Oct 7, 2016

Konta unsettled Pliskova, of the Czech Republic, with clever use of the drop shot and agile net play.

Pliskova is known for blowing hot and cold and while her heavy serve was almost unplayable in the second set, her often error strewn groundstrokes returned to haunt her in the decider.
