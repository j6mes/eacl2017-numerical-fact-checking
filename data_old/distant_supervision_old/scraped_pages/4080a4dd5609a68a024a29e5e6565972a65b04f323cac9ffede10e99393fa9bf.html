This is the Hottest Female Star In the World.

Every year Maxim Magazine presents a list of the world’s most beautiful women. Over the past years the ladies who topped to list had a tendency to wear little to no clothing. But this year we are very surprised with the outcome, new Editor in Chief Kate Lanphear decided it was time to look at woman differently. Beauty to her is more than just looks, relevancy and talent count as well. That sounds very promising to us!

According to Maxim men Taylor Swift totally fits the profile and shines on this year’s #1 spot! To Maxim Taylor is the quintessential American success story. She worked very hard to rise from that storied Pennsylvania Christmas-tree farm to the covers of magazines. The young poster is breaking album sales records while retaining the same wide-eyed charm that made her so likable in the first place.

When Maxim asks Taylor what she thinks of being named no.1 she says: ‘Its really nice and such an incredible compliment. This year has been my favorite year of my life so far. I got to make an album exactly the way I wanted to make it. I got to put it out exactly the way that I dreamed of putting it out. Every one of these kinds of whims and ideas came to fruition. The videosIm proud of those, Im proud of the tour, Im proud of the way this has all happened. In the midst of all that, this is really nice. It really feels like a wonderful celebration of my favorite year.’

Things are looking up for the blonde singer, keep up the good work Swift!
