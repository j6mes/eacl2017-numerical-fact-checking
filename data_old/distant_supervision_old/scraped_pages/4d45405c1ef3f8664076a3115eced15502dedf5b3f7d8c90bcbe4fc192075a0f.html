  • U.K. Elite Soccer Summer Tournament Teams

    Our Summer Tournament Teams provide players aged U9-U14 an opportunity to play competitive soccer through June and July. This player centered program challenges individuals to become effective in game situations through bi-weekly training sessions and 2-3 regional tournaments.
