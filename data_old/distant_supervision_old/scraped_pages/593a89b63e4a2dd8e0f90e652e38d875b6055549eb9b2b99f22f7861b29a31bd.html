Queens Park Rangers FC

Queens Park Rangers FC was founded in 1886 after the merger of Christchurch Rangers and St. Judes Institute, and their traditional colours are blue and white. They currently play in the Championship. Their nickname is The Hoops and home ground is Loftus Road.
