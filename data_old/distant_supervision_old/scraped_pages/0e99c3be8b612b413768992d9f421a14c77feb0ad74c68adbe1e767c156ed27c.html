Middlesbrough

Middlesbrough were formed in 1876 and played much of their football, from 1903-1995 before they moved to the Riverside Stadium. Their only major honour came in 2004 when they won the League Cup, and their last spell in the top flight lasted 11 years, which ended in 2009.
