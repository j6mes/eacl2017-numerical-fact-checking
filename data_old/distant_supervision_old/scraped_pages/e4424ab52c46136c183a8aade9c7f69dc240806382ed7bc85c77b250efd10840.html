American Football

  • Includes NFL, NCAA College Football, CFL and Arena Football.
  • At least 55 minutes of play must elapse for bets to have action, unless otherwise stated.
  • Overtime counts for bets unless otherwise specified.
  • Abandoned or postponed matches are void with the exception of the Super Bowl final when all bets will stand whenever the game is played.
  • All results as per the official governing bodies – the official Game Book provided at www.nfl.com, www.ncaa.org, www.nfleurope.com, www.cfl.ca, and www.arenafootball.com.

1. General Types of Wager

Point spreads and totals may be offered on the 1st, 2nd, 3rd or 4th quarters, the 1st or 2nd half, or the whole game. A money line is usually only offered on full game wagers. Full game bets on these types of general wagers include any overtime.
‘Push’ result – Stakes on straight/single bets are returned, and in parlays/multiples the selection is treated as a non-runner, and the bet will be settled on the remaining selections.

2. Money Line

Both teams are priced to win the full game with no handicaps applied. When wagering on the money line your team must win the game on the field in order for you to win your bet. For settlement purposes overtime counts. If after Overtime the scores are still tied bets will be “pushed”.

3. Point Spread

A point spread is a bet on a team to win the game with the handicaps applied. To even the match for betting purposes, the favourite gives the underdog a handicap start. A favourite is indicated by a minus points handicap and the underdog by plus points handicap. At the end of the relevant period of play, the relevant handicap is added to, or subtracted from the selected teams score to determine the point spread result. Where teams are deemed to be evenly matched, no point spread is applied; this is called a ‘pick’ game. When scores are level after accounting for any handicap the result is a ‘push’.

4. Game Totals

A total line is offered on the predicted total number of points scored. Prices are offered for the actual score to be over or under this quote. The total result is the combined total points for both teams at the end of the relevant period of play. When the combined scores of both team's equals the total line exactly then the result is a ‘push’. Overtime counts.

5. Game Wagers

At least 55 minutes of play must elapse for game bets to have action.

6. Quarter/1st Half Wagers

The relevant quarter or half must be completed for bets to have action. The 4th quarter does not include overtime.

7. 2nd Half Wagers

The 2nd half must be completed in full to have action. Overtime counts.

8. In Game Wagers

Normal US football game rules apply for spread/total/money line bets. If a game spread/total/money line is quoted, it is for the whole game including any overtime played. Please note: as with all in-play betting markets, the market may be closed at any time.

9. Point Spread/Total Points In-Play

This market is offered on selected games with point spread lines available and updated whilst the game is in-play. The Total Points quoted is for the whole game including overtime if played, and the result is the combined total points for both teams. Push rules apply. However, the spread in-play may contain a “tie” option.

10. Game Props

Normal US football game rules apply for spread/total/money line bets. If a game spread/total/money line is quoted, it is for the whole game including any overtime played. All bets are action for prop bets such as ‘team to score next’ and ‘next scoring play is’. Please note: as with all in-play betting markets, the market may be closed at any time.

Propositions or ‘props’ are additional betting opportunities offered on the game. They can cover a wide range of results and occurrences within the game, e.g., total offensive yards, total first downs, longest scoring play, time of first score, etc.

Any special game proposition bets are accepted single bets only, unless specified otherwise.

Overtime counts for all proposition bets unless specifically stated otherwise. For example, overtime does not count for double result, highest scoring quarter of the game and half with most points.

The period of play necessary for bets to have action may vary from prop to prop. Customers are advised to liaise with customer services if necessary.
  • ‘Team to score first’ – Prices are offered on each team to score first. In the event that no team scores (including Overtime) bets will be voided.
  • ‘Team to score last’ – Prices are offered on each team to score last. In the event that no team scores (including Overtime) bets will be voided. At least 55 minutes of game play must have elapsed for bets to have action.
  • ‘A score in the final two minutes of the 1st half’ –The 1st half must be completed for bets to have action, unless either team has already scored in the final two minutes of the 1st half at the time the game was suspended.
  • ‘Who will score first in the 2nd half’ – At least 55 minutes of game play must have elapsed for bets to have action, unless a team has already scored in the 2nd half at the time the game was suspended. If the 2nd half produces 2 (zero) game points then all bets are void.
  • ‘A score in the final three minutes 30 seconds of the 2nd half’ – At least 60 minutes of game pay must have elapsed for bets to have action, unless either team has already scored in the final three minutes 30 seconds of the 2nd half at the time the game was suspended.
  • ‘Will either team score three unanswered times’ – Forecast whether either team will score three unanswered times during the game. A ‘score’ excludes any PAT's (point after touchdown or two point conversions). At least 55 minutes of game play must have elapsed for bets to have action, unless either team has already scored three unanswered times at the time the game was suspended.
  • ‘Double result’ –Correctly forecast both the result of the game at half time and the result of the game at the end of the 2nd half, i.e., 1st and 2nd half scores added together. At least 55 minutes of game play must have elapsed for bets to have action. Overtime does not count.
  • ‘Winning margin’ –A market offered on a game to predict the winning team and the margin of victory. At least 55 minutes of game play must have elapsed for bets to have action.
  • ‘Highest scoring quarter’ –A market offered on which quarter of the game will produce the highest score. In the event of a tie, the dead-heat rules apply. At least 60 minutes of game play must have elapsed for bets to have action. Overtime does not count. Dead heat rule applies
  • ‘First scoring play’ –A market offered on which team will record the first score of the game and what the pay will be. At least 55 minutes before game play must have elapsed for bets to have action, unless a team has already scored at the time the game was suspended.
  • ‘Team totals’ – At least 55 minutes of game play must have elapsed for bet to have action, unless a teams total has already gone 'over' the number quoted.
  • ‘Shortest touchdown scored’ –At least 55 minutes of game play must have elapsed for bets to have action, unless a touchdown has already been scored that was ‘under” the number quoted.
  • ‘Longest field goal of the game’ –At least 55 minutes of game play must have elapsed for bets to have action, unless a field goal has already been scored that was ‘over’ the number quoted.
  • ‘Longest touchdown of the game’ – At least 55 minutes of game play must have elapsed for bets to have action, unless a touchdown has already been scored that was over the number.
  • ‘Time of first score’ – At least 55 minutes of game play must have elapsed for bets to have action, unless a team has already scored.
  • ‘Half with most points’ – At least 55 minutes of game play must have elapsed for bets to have action. Overtime does not count.

The following additional game props will require at least 55 minutes of game play for bets to have action and include overtime if played (regardless of whether or not the prop has gone over or under the number quoted before 55 minutes has been played):

total plays, total offensive yards, total first downs, total rushing yards, total rushing attempts, total passing yards, total pass attempts, total pass completions, total sacks, total turnovers, total penalties, total punts, total field goals made, team to score longest touchdown, team to score longest field goal

Wagering is available on the performance of a named player in a variety of achievements, e.g., pass yards, receptions, tackles and assists. Overtime counts for all proposition bets/match-ups unless specifically stated otherwise. All prop bets are accepted single bets only, unless stated otherwise.

14. Futures/Season Bets

  • NFL Divisional winners are determined by games won during regular season (NFL tie break rules apply), based on teams completing all 16 games.

15. Outright Betting

Prices are offered on teams or players to be outright winners of a given tournament or competition. For example, the Super Bowl is the outright tournament in NFL. The Super Bowl final winners are the outright tournament winners. Prices are offered for each team participating.

16. Conference/Division Betting

Teams participating in an outright tournament may be grouped in conferences and/or divisions. For example, NFL is grouped into two conferences (American Football Conference and National Football Conference), each containing four divisions (North, East, West and South). Prices are offered for each participating team to win their respective conference and division.

Boxing

1. Outright Betting

A price will be offered for the 'draw' and in the event of this occurring, all bets on either boxer to win will be losers. If the verdict is a "Technical Draw" the result will be deemed as a draw.

2. Round Betting

If, for any reason, a points decision is awarded before the full number of rounds are completed, bets will be settled on the round in which the fight was stopped. Bets which nominated 'to win on points' will only be deemed winners if the full number of rounds are completed.

If a boxer fails to answer the bell, the fight will be deemed to have ended in the previous round, irrespective of what the official decision may be.

If, for any reason the selected number of rounds on which we are betting is changed, all round by round betting will be void, but match bets will stand.

Bets will be settled on the official result immediately following the fight. Announcements or enquiries after this result will not affect settlement.

Prices may be offered for a fight to be won by KO (includes TKO, disqualification), decision (includes Technical Decision) or draw. Bets which nominated 'to win by decision` will only be deemed winners if the full number of rounds are completed.

3. Postponed/cancelled bout.

If a contest is postponed and rescheduled to take place within 48 hours of the original start time, your bet on that contest will stand unless cancelled by mutual consent. If the contest does not take place within 48 hours your bet will be void and stakes returned unless included in an accumulator or other multiple wager when your selection will be treated as a non-runner and the stake will run onto the remaining selections in the bet.
