IGL Retriever Championship

  
Winner 2015 - FTCh. Asterix Aguzannis of Chatsworth. Owned by Lady Celina Carter handled by John Halsted Jnr

View all pictures
 

The 93rd International Gundog League Retriever Championship took place at Queensberry Estate, Dumfries and Galloway, Scotland by generous invitation of His Grace The Duke of Buccleuch and Queensberry on 6 - 9 December 2016. 

The Judges were Mr N T Rowson (118), Mr N S Black (299), Mr M J Tallamy (1925) and Mr S Polley (2435)﻿

The 2015 Championship Results were: 

1st Place - FTCh. Asterix Aguzannis of Chatsworth.  Owned by Lady Celina Carter, handled by John Halsted Jnr.

2nd Place - FTCh Rimrock Hurricane owned and handled by Andy Latham

3rd Place - Carltonstar Toby owned and handled by Pete Goddard

Diplomas of Merit:
Garronpoint Avoca owned and handled by Annette Clark.
INT FTCh Waygreen Apollo Int Ftch owned and handled by Nigel Carville.
FTCh Snowdell Spender owned and handled by Stephanie Smith.
FTCh Waterford Ganton owned and handled by Jayne Coley.
Amancio Falcon owned and handled by Sinead McMillan
FTCh Brockweir Daisy owned and handled by Di Wiggins.
FTCh Emmanygan Rambler owned by the Duchess of Devonshire, handled by John Halsted Jnr.﻿
